🏷️ Issue Categories & Booking Time

Categories shown on the IT Support form. Minutes is how long that job normally takes — it's the calendar slot the appointment books. Priority score is the starting score for the Smart Priority engine (higher = more urgent).

⏱️ Time Overrides

Words in the ticket description that override the category's minutes. A longer-job match always wins over a quicker one, so "set up new laptop" books 4 hours even under a 90-minute category. Separate words with commas.

🎯 Smart Priority Keywords

The form scores each ticket: it starts at the category's priority score, then every matching word below adds its band's weight. Higher totals mean higher urgency. Put one word or phrase per line — matching is case-insensitive.

Totals: 90+ → Critical  ·  50–89 → High  ·  20–49 → Medium  ·  under 20 → Low. Use a negative weight (like the Low band) to reduce urgency.
